Custard Tart

Caloric and weight loss benefits of custard Tarts

detailed description

Pastry part: 75g sugar 150 g softened butter 300g medium gluten flour 25g egg 3 G vanilla sugar powder

1。 Beat the sugar and butter until the color becomes lighter, about 2-3 minutes

2。 Add half the eggs and vanilla sugar powder that have been scattered. Stir well.

3。 Add flour, stir, and then knead it into dough by hand. And put it aside and let it rest for at least 20 minutes.

4。 Prepare the movable tower formwork.

5。 Roll the rest dough into 5mm thick round pieces with rolling stick.

6。 Wrap the crispy piece on the rolling stick and support it to the tower mold. Gently let the tard stick around the mold.

Kashda filling: 150 g Sugar 300 g egg 600 ml milk 5g sweet sugar powder 2 g nutmeg powder

1。 Preheat the oven to 170 ° C. Put the sugar and eggs in a bowl. Beat with a manual beater until most of the sugar dissolves. For 1-2 minutes, don't pass it.

2。 Add milk and vanilla sugar. Stir well.

3。 Pour the mixture into the pastry, close to the top of the tare. Sprinkle some nutmeg powder.

4。 Bake the tower in the oven for about 40 minutes until the pie is shaken and the filling will not move.

5。 Wait half an hour to cool down the tower and take the tower mold around, and you can eat it.
